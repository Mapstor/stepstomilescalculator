Accuracy

We strive for accuracy in our calculations based on established formulas and methodologies. The calculator uses the following approach:

  • Stride length is estimated as a percentage of height (walking: 41.5%, running: 52%)
  • Distance is calculated using the formula: Miles = (Steps × Stride Length) ÷ 5,280
  • These formulas represent population averages from biomechanical research

However, we do not guarantee the accuracy of results. Individual stride length varies based on factors including leg length, walking speed, terrain, fitness level, and gait patterns. For precise measurements, use GPS tracking devices or measured courses.

The formulas and methodology used are documented on our About page.
